I'm Lea P!

Lea P offers kiteboarding instruction for beginner and intermediate level kiters. She has a methodical and safety-driven teaching style that uses a building block approach to incrementally improve your understanding and performance. Lea primarly instructs through Real Watersports in Waves, NC.

Real Watersports

Real Watersports began in 2001 and created their trademark camp Zero To Hero after their training sessions with Lea 2001. They have since become the premier training facililty in the world for kiteboarding.
